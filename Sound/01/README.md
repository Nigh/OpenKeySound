
## Sound Types  
- `click`: Press and release the key immediately  
- `press`: From pressing the key to actuation  
- `bottom`: Pressing the key to the bottom after actuation  
- `release`: Releasing the key after bottoming out

## 声音类型
- `click`: 按下按键立即松开
- `press`: 按下按键到触发
- `bottom`: 触发按键后按压到底
- `release`: 触底后松开按键
